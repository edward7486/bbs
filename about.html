About Bed Bug Survivors

Bed Bug Survivors is dedicated to sharing the human experience of dealing with bed bug infestations. Our goal is to spread knowledge, awareness, and support for those who have encountered these relentless pests.

Our Story

The inspiration for this site comes from my own personal battle with bed bugs. It all started when I unknowingly brought them into my tiny studio apartment by purchasing a used futon from Craigslist. What followed was an exhausting struggle that affected not just my home, but also my mental well-being. Even after eliminating the infestation, the psychological toll lingered. Over a decade later, this site serves as my way of fighting back—by educating others and providing a platform for survivors to share their stories.
