Request Removal of a Bed Bug Report

Want to request a report removal?

We understand that bed bug accusations can be serious and sometimes incorrect. That's why we offer a simple, respectful way to request removal of a report you believe is inaccurate, resolved, or submitted in bad faith.

Submitting a removal request helps ensure the long-term integrity, fairness, and credibility of our public reporting map.

Please provide as much information as possible about the report in question — including the title, address, and date of the listing, if available. Tell us who you are (tenant, building owner, manager, etc.) and why you're requesting the removal.

We manually review all removal requests. Submitting a request does not guarantee deletion. In some cases, we may reach out for more details and appreciate your patience in resolving this matter.
