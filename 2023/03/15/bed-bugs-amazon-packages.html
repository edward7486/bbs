Yes, you can get bed bugs from Amazon packages

If this Reddit post on r/bedbugs is any indication, it is absolutely possible to get bed bugs through a delivered package, even Amazon.

You might think this is unlikely but Amazon has had to close down entire warehouses due to bed bug infestations so it is not as uncommon as you may think.

Throw launderable items in the dryer right away

Even though it’s’unlikely, kill any bed bugs by throwing newly opened items in the dryer if possible. I’ve put backpacks, clothes, and shoes in the dryer many times to make sure I wasn’t carrying bed bugs out of my house everyday.
