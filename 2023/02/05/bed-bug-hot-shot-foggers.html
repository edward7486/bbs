Bed Bug Foggers didn't work (for me)

When I first discovered my bed bug infestation I researched what bed bugs were and how others dealt with them. One method that sounded promising was to use a bed bug fogger. It sounds like the perfect solution, release the fog and done. What a naive way to think with bed bugs! I tried the fogger three times and it never made a difference in a tiny 400 sq ft. studio apartment. I suspect foggers do not work as well in older apartments with deep cracks or crevices that allow bed bugs to harbor deep in the walls, which was my situation.

But the bed bug foggers on Amazon have 4 star reviews?

Amazon’s marketplace is highly competitive and products notoriously have fake reviews to drive their ratings up. If you are doing DIY bed bug treatment, be more pragmatic with your purchases and do not rely solely on a rating alone. Be sure to read the critical reviews. Many negative reviews for foggers mention they could in fact make your infestation worse by driving the bed bugs further into your home as they escape the fog.
